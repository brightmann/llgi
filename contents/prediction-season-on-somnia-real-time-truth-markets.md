## somnia data streams — the engine behind prediction season

in **october 2025**, somnia launched **Data Streams**, a subscription-based infrastructure that lets developers tap into **structured, verifiable, real-time on-chain event feeds**.

instead of polling or relying on heavy indexers, developers can **subscribe to live data streams** such as:

- esports match outcomes  
- live sports performance  
- financial instrument movements  
- DAO governance results  
- AI model performance metrics  
- in-game events  
- social and sentiment signals  

this transforms prediction markets from delayed systems into **fully reactive, live intelligence platforms**.
